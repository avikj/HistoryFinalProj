Congratulations! You have been proclaimed as the Emperor of Japan. Unfortunately, World War II has just begun with the German invasion of Poland. The leader of the Third Reich, Adolf Hitler, is a ruthless and power-hungry man. Surprisingly, Hitler has shown interest in allying with Japan and hopes to plan and carry out operations with you in the near future. Japanese intelligence, however, believes Hitler may be trying to double-cross you and your country. As the new emperor, it is essential you keep the people on your side. They must respect you and your actions in order for you to maintain your leadership. Choose the best course of action as you lead Japan through the midst of World War II. Good luck!
